ABOUT DimeX

DimeX is a decentralized staking and liquidity ecosystem built with a liquidity-first approach to establish strong token fundamentals before expansion. The platform focuses on structured growth, transparent reward distribution, and sustainable ecosystem development. Users participate by staking assets, directly contributing to liquidity strength while earning rewards for supporting the network. By prioritizing liquidity and stability, DimeX creates a scalable environment designed for long-term growth and market confidence.

WHY STAKING?

DimeX staking is designed with a liquidity-first approach to strengthen market stability and support long-term token value.

  • Users can stake USDT or DMX within the platform
  • Staked assets contribute directly to liquidity growth
  • Liquidity is integrated into market pools to improve stability
  • Stronger liquidity helps reduce volatility and build confidence
  • Participants receive rewards for supporting the ecosystem

This model creates a structured cycle where user participation strengthens liquidity, and stronger liquidity supports long-term token stability and growth.

HOW REWARDS WORK

Participants who stake receive rewards in DMX based on their contribution and staking duration.

  • Rewards can be held for long-term value
  • Swapped through decentralized exchanges like PancakeSwap
  • Converted into other supported assets
  • Re-staked to compound earnings over time

This flexible reward system allows users to manage their earnings based on individual strategies while supporting ecosystem growth.

Income Cap (4X Limit)

To maintain sustainability, DimeX applies a 4X income cap.

  • You can earn up to 4 times your total stake
  • This includes: Direct income + Level income + Team Bonus + Rewards
  • Once 4X is reached, earnings will stop automatically
  • Re-staking resets your earning cycle
